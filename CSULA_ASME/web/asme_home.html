About Us

ASME is a national organization based on mechanical engineering and its foundations. Here at Cal State LA's charter club, we provide workshops, competition, and resources for all club members of all majors. We have projects and experiences that provide great talking points on resumes and job applications! We plan to compete in the C-UASC (CSU California Unmanned Aerial Systems Competition at Mojave Air & Space Port) and the MathWorks drone competition, where we will be using Computer Science, Electrical Engineering and Mechanical Engineering concepts to build our drone.
